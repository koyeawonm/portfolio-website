Background

The space between
technology and people

I'm a product marketer and GTM strategist who thrives at the intersection of technology and human behavior. My career has been defined by a single question: how do you make complex technology feel essential to the people who need it?

At Niantic, I spent five years shaping go-to-market strategy for Pokémon GO — one of the world's most-played mobile games — leading campaigns that generated over a billion impressions and some of the largest DAU surges in the game's history.

At NVIDIA, I applied that lens to Agentic AI — identifying adoption barriers across 25+ AI partners and designing enablement architecture to reduce friction across NVIDIA's ecosystem.

Today, I'm completing my Wharton MBA with majors in Neuromarketing and AI in Business, deepening my understanding of why people adopt (or resist) new technologies.
